# Deep Learning Question Bank

## 1. Why does batch normalization help?

- It stabilizes intermediate activations and often allows faster, more reliable training.

## 2. Why do residual connections matter?

- They make optimization easier in deep networks by preserving a gradient-friendly path.

## 3. Why can validation loss rise while training loss falls?

- That usually signals overfitting.

## 4. What would you check if training is unstable?

- learning rate
- normalization
- exploding gradients
- bad initialization
- label noise

## 5. CNN vs transformer for vision

- CNNs often win on efficiency and inductive bias.
- Transformers can scale better with data and pretraining.
