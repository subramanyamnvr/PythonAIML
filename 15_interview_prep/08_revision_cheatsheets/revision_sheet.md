# Revision Cheatsheet

## Metrics

- precision: when false positives are expensive
- recall: when false negatives are expensive
- F1: when both matter
- ROC-AUC: ranking across thresholds
- PR-AUC: better when positives are rare

## GenAI Checks

- retrieval quality
- citation quality
- eval coverage
- latency budget
- guardrail coverage

## Interview Loop

- answer briefly
- expand with tradeoffs
- give one example
- mention one failure mode
